About Us

Who are we?

ZUNJ Divyang Sanstha is a dedicated NGO committed to empowering individuals with disabilities by providing essential support, resources, and opportunities. Through advocacy, skill development, and inclusive programs, ZUNJ strives to enhance the quality of life for the disabled community, fostering independence and ensuring equal participation in society.
